# Module Catalog - Interview Prep System

## Core Modules

### 1. Early Life
- **Description**: Childhood, family background, formative experiences
- **Required for**: All tiers
- **Default variant**: Standard
- **Template**: Structured narrative with key milestones
- **AI Prompt**: "Extract information about childhood, family, education, and early influences that shaped their later career"

### 2. Timeline
- **Description**: Chronological list of significant life and career events
- **Required for**: All tiers
- **Default variant**: Expanded
- **Template**: Date-based entries with context
- **AI Prompt**: "Create a comprehensive timeline of major life events, career milestones, and significant achievements"

### 3. Key Quotes
- **Description**: Most impactful and representative quotes
- **Required for**: All tiers
- **Default variant**: Standard
- **Template**: Quote with source and context
- **AI Prompt**: "Identify memorable quotes that capture their philosophy, vision, or personality"

### 4. Career Highlights
- **Description**: Major professional achievements and contributions
- **Required for**: Standard, Premium
- **Default variant**: Standard
- **Template**: Achievement-based sections
- **AI Prompt**: "Summarize key career accomplishments, innovations, and industry impact"

### 5. Leadership Style
- **Description**: Management philosophy and leadership approach
- **Required for**: Premium
- **Default variant**: Standard
- **Template**: Thematic analysis with examples
- **AI Prompt**: "Analyze their leadership style, management philosophy, and team-building approach"

### 6. Innovation Philosophy
- **Description**: Approach to creativity and innovation
- **Required for**: Premium (Tech/Creator stamps)
- **Default variant**: Standard
- **Template**: Conceptual framework with case studies
- **AI Prompt**: "Examine their approach to innovation, creative process, and breakthrough thinking"

### 7. Controversies & Challenges
- **Description**: Significant controversies, failures, and how they were handled
- **Required for**: Premium
- **Default variant**: Minimal
- **Template**: Balanced narrative with multiple perspectives
- **AI Prompt**: "Document major controversies or challenges faced, including context and resolution"

### 8. Personal Life
- **Description**: Family, hobbies, interests outside of work
- **Required for**: Standard, Premium
- **Default variant**: Minimal
- **Template**: Respectful overview of personal aspects
- **AI Prompt**: "Provide appropriate personal life details that offer insight into character"

### 9. Legacy & Impact
- **Description**: Long-term influence and contributions
- **Required for**: All tiers
- **Default variant**: Standard
- **Template**: Analysis of lasting impact
- **AI Prompt**: "Assess their lasting legacy and ongoing influence in their field"

### 10. Quick Facts
- **Description**: Rapid-fire facts and trivia
- **Required for**: Express tier
- **Default variant**: Minimal
- **Template**: Bulleted list format
- **AI Prompt**: "Generate interesting, lesser-known facts suitable for conversation starters"

### 11. Media Appearances
- **Description**: Notable interviews, speeches, and public appearances
- **Required for**: Premium
- **Default variant**: Standard
- **Template**: Chronological list with key takeaways
- **AI Prompt**: "Catalog significant media appearances with context and notable moments"

### 12. Published Works
- **Description**: Books, articles, patents, or other publications
- **Required for**: Standard, Premium (Author stamp)
- **Default variant**: Expanded
- **Template**: Bibliography with summaries
- **AI Prompt**: "List all published works with brief descriptions and reception"

### 13. Awards & Recognition
- **Description**: Honors, awards, and formal recognition
- **Required for**: All tiers
- **Default variant**: Standard
- **Template**: Chronological list with significance
- **AI Prompt**: "Document all major awards and recognition received"

### 14. Key Relationships
- **Description**: Important professional and personal relationships
- **Required for**: Premium
- **Default variant**: Standard
- **Template**: Network analysis with context
- **AI Prompt**: "Identify key relationships that influenced their career and life"

### 15. Financial Impact
- **Description**: Economic influence and financial legacy
- **Required for**: Premium (Public Company stamp)
- **Default variant**: Expanded
- **Template**: Data-driven analysis
- **AI Prompt**: "Analyze financial impact, wealth creation, and economic influence"

## Module Variants

### Standard
- Comprehensive coverage
- 500-800 words per module
- Multiple sources required
- Full citation

### Expanded
- In-depth analysis
- 800-1500 words per module
- Extensive sourcing
- Additional context and analysis

### Minimal
- Essential information only
- 200-400 words per module
- Key points focus
- Streamlined citations

## Tier Requirements

### Express Tier
- Early Life (Minimal)
- Timeline (Minimal)
- Key Quotes (Minimal)
- Quick Facts (Standard)
- Awards & Recognition (Minimal)

### Standard Tier
- All Express modules plus:
- Career Highlights (Standard)
- Personal Life (Minimal)
- Published Works (Standard)
- Legacy & Impact (Standard)

### Premium Tier
- All Standard modules plus:
- Leadership Style (Standard)
- Innovation Philosophy (Standard)
- Controversies & Challenges (Minimal)
- Media Appearances (Standard)
- Key Relationships (Standard)
- Financial Impact (if applicable)
